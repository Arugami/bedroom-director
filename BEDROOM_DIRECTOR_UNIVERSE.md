**"Hollywood Studios Florida noir meets Miami 80s twilight"** - The complete visual system:

**The Feeling:** That moment when you pull into Universal Studios at dusk - the sun just set, palm trees frame the purple sky, neon signs glow, and you're about to experience something magical. Cinematic + nostalgic, sophisticated + emotional, tropical + moody.

**The DNA:** Scarface ambition + Drive aesthetics + Miami Vice rebellion + Hollywood Tower elegance + Blockbuster confidence + bedroom creator accessibility.

**Color Palette:**
- **Primary:** Director Black (#000000), Bedroom Purple (#7C3AED), Purple Bloom (#8B5CF6)
- **Accent Neons:** Retro Teal (#00CED1), Sunset Orange (#FF8C42), Neon Yellow (#FCD34D)
- **Atmospheric:** Twilight sky gradients (purple/blue), warm window glows (orange/yellow)

**Visual Style:**
- **Palm tree silhouettes** - Signature framing element (Hollywood/Florida/Miami noir aesthetic)
- **Practical neon lighting** - Real glowing effects, colored uplighting, visible halos
- **Twilight atmosphere** - Purple/blue gradient skies, dusk/golden hour mood
- **Multi-color storytelling** - Cool tones (purple/blue) + warm accents (yellow/orange/green)
- **Theatrical presentation** - Like entering a theme park attraction
- **Cinematic depth** - Film grain, atmospheric haze, layered lighting
- **90s theme park elegance** - Hollywood Tower Hotel sophistication meets Blockbuster confidence

**Signature Visual Elements:**
1. **Palm Trees** - ESSENTIAL brand DNA. Silhouettes against twilight skies create the Hollywood/Florida/Miami noir aesthetic. Not just decoration - they frame every scene and connect the brand to California dreams, Florida theme parks, and 80s Miami rebellion. Think: Scarface, Drive, Universal Studios Orlando at dusk.

4. **Director's Chair** - Symbol of the bedroom director
5. **Practical Lighting** - LED glow, colored uplighting, warm window glows

**NOT:** Cheesy VHS glitches, over-the-top effects, flat modern minimalism, garish colors

- Neon sign lettering (glowing outlines, halos)
