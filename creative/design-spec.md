# Bedroom Director - Design Specification

**Version:** 3.0  
**Last Updated:** November 4, 2025  
**Brand:** bedroomdirector.com  
**Tagline:** "From bedroom to big screen"

**Project Goal:** Empower bedroom creators to discover, learn, and master AI creative tools. Guide filmmakers and content creators to the RIGHT tool for their vision, with proven techniques from the community. We're not just a directory â€” we're a movement for creative sovereignty.

**Creative Direction:** Hybrid approach (W+K rebellion + Chiat/Day reverence + Jobs clarity)

---

## Core Philosophy

### Design Principles
1. **Intent-Based Navigation** - "What do you want to create?" not "Browse categories"
2. **Empowerment Over Gatekeeping** - No jargon, no barriers, no permission needed
3. **Taste Over Technology** - Show craft, not code; artistry, not algorithms
4. **Progressive Disclosure** - Simple for beginners, deep for experts
5. **Platform Transparency** - Show WHERE to use tools + pricing comparison
6. **Community Wisdom** - Real creators, real results, real techniques
7. **Always Actionable** - Every page: Discover â†’ Learn â†’ Create (elsewhere) â†’ Share
8. **Rebellion + Reverence** - Raw authenticity meets cinematic elegance

### Target Audience
- **Primary:** Bedroom Directors (indie filmmakers, content creators, aspiring directors)
- **Secondary:** Creative agencies and marketing teams
- **Tertiary:** Film students and serious hobbyists

**Not:** Enterprise users, developers, technical researchers (they have other tools)

## Creative Direction Guidelines

### **When to Use Each Voice:**

**W+K (Rebellion) - Social Media, Community:**
- Urgent, defiant, inclusive
- "We don't wait" messaging
- Fast-paced, energetic
- Example: "Join the rebellion"

**Chiat/Day (Reverence) - Brand Content, Showcases:**
- Elegant, philosophical, minimal
- "This is where it begins" messaging
- Slow, contemplative
- Example: "Your bedroom is your studio"

**Jobs (Clarity) - Product Updates, Announcements:**
- Simple, clear, emotional
- "One more thing" surprises
- Direct, human
- Example: "Available now"

**Hybrid (Main Brand) - Homepage, Campaigns:**
- Rebellion + Reverence + Clarity
- "The new Hollywood is everywhere"
- Balanced, comprehensive
- Example: Hero section above

### **Content Rhythm:**
- **Monday:** W+K energy (Tool of the Week)
- **Wednesday:** Chiat/Day reverence (Director Spotlight)
- **Friday:** Jobs clarity (This Week in AI Filmmaking)
- **Major Launches:** Hybrid approach

## Steve Jobs' 5 Persuasion Principles for Website Copy

*These principles should guide all website copy, features, and messaging.*

### **1. The Labor Illusion**
**Principle:** "If you see the labor going into a task, you value the product more."

**For Website:**
- Show the process, not just final results
- "How they made it" breakdowns showing:
  - Late-night generations
  - Prompt tuning iterations  
  - Version 3s that failed
  - Micro-tweaks to make it feel "human"

**Example Copy:**
```
"This shot took 47 generations.
Not because AI is bad.
But because great filmmaking is about taste, not speed.

Bedroom Director helps you find that 47th generation faster.
But the taste? That's all you."
```

---

### **2. The Halo Effect**
**Principle:** Jobs linked Apple with Einstein, Gandhi, Lennon â€” "Think Different."

**For Website:**
- Feature modern creative heroes (bedroom filmmakers, AI artists, one-person studios)
- Create "Think Different" moment for bedroom directors

**Example Copy (About Page):**
```
"Here's to the Bedroom Directors.

The ones who turn prompts into pictures.
The ones who light scenes with imagination, not spotlights.
The ones who know that great stories don't need permission.

They're not waiting for Hollywood.
They're building the next Hollywood.

Bedroom Director."
```

---

### **3. Anchoring**
**Principle:** Jobs reframed iPod's $399 as "$0.30 per song" â€” compete on context, not price.

**For Website:**
- Anchor against film school, Hollywood budgets, traditional crews
- NOT against other tool directories

**Example Copy (Homepage):**
```
Film school: $80,000
Studio camera: $40,000  
Knowing which AI tools can make your film tonight: Free
```

**Or (Comparison Section):**
```
Traditional production:
6-person crew, 3-day shoot, $50K budget

Bedroom Director:
1 creator, 1 night, Wi-Fi
```

---

### **4. Chunking**  
**Principle:** Jobs grouped iPhone into "three revolutionary products" â€” then revealed it's one.

**For Website:**
- Group ecosystem into three acts, then reveal they're one system

**Example Copy (About/Launch Page):**
```
"Today, we're introducing three revolutionary things:

One: A comprehensive guide to every AI creative tool.
Two: A community where filmmakers share techniques that actually work.
Three: A platform that shows you what's possible when you remove the limits.

[Pause]

But these aren't three separate things.

They're one creative system.

Bedroom Director."
```

---

### **5. Recency Bias + 'One More Thing'**
**Principle:** Jobs always ended with a surprise to leave the last emotional hit strong.

**For Website:**
- End launches with surprise reveals
- New tool sections
- Cinematic drops from creators  
- Partnership announcements

**Example (Launch Announcements):**
```
"Oh, and one more thing...

We've partnered with [major AI model] to bring you
exclusive access to [new feature].

Available only on Bedroom Director."
```
