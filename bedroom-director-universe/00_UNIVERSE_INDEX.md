## üéØ Core Principles

### **1. Everything Feels Real**
- Products have manufacturing details (Hudson County, NJ)
- Locations have addresses (even if fictional)
- Films have release dates and runtimes
- Everything has a "founded in 1989" backstory

### **2. 90s/Early 2000s Aesthetic**
- VHS grain and film artifacts
- Practical effects over CGI
- Neon signage and LED strips
- Disposable camera quality
- Theme park attraction energy

### **3. Bedroom Creator Ethos**
- "No Hollywood Required"
- "From bedroom to big screen"
- DIY, independent, rebellious
- Accessible, democratic, empowering

### **4. Cinematic Universe Logic**
- Everything connects
- Cross-references between products/locations
- Shared timeline and history
- Easter eggs and callbacks

## üìù Contributing to the Universe

When adding new elements:

1. **Create a new markdown file** in the appropriate folder
2. **Follow the naming convention:** `ELEMENT_NAME.md` (uppercase, underscores)
3. **Include these sections:**
   - Overview
   - History (how it fits in the 1989-2025 timeline)
   - Details (specifics about the product/location/film)
   - Visual Identity (colors, typography, design)
   - Cross-References (how it connects to other universe elements)
4. **Update this index** with a link and brief description
5. **Maintain consistency** with existing universe elements
