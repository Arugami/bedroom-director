## The Timeline

### **1989 - The Founding (Fictional)**
In a basement studio in Hudson County, NJ, bedroom creators needed fuel for their all-night sessions. Bedroom Fuel‚Ñ¢ was born. Not in a lab. Not in a boardroom. In a bedroom.

### **1990s - The Analog Era**
Bedroom creators fueled by passion (and Bedroom Fuel) made independent films, produced hip-hop beats, and animated frame by frame. The DIY revolution was underway.

### **2000s - The Digital Transition**
Cameras got cheaper. Software got accessible. YouTube launched. The bedroom director movement grew.

### **2010s - The Democratization**
Smartphones became cameras. Editing software became free. Distribution became global. Bedroom directors went mainstream.

### **2020s - The AI Revolution**
AI tools democratized every aspect of production. But with 1000+ tools, creators needed guidance. Bedroom Director was born (the website, not just the drink).

### **2025 - Today**
10,000+ bedroom directors. 156+ AI tools cataloged. A growing universe. The movement is real.
